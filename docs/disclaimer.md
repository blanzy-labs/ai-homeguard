# Disclaimer

AI HomeGuard is a local defensive cyber hygiene tool for home users.

It is not a penetration test, security certification, vulnerability scanner certification, legal service, security consulting service, compliance assessment, or guarantee of safety.

Security findings, AI-assisted explanations, and defensive guidance may be incomplete, incorrect, outdated, or not applicable to a specific environment.

D3FEND-informed guidance in AI HomeGuard is a small local educational mapping inspired by defensive concepts and D3FEND-style countermeasure thinking. It is not official D3FEND coverage, not a complete ontology implementation, not professional security or compliance advice, and not proof that a device, account, or network is secure.

Local network awareness is informational only. It uses passive local context and may be incomplete, especially in Docker or constrained runtimes. It is not a penetration test, vulnerability assessment certification, device inventory guarantee, router audit, or proof that a network is secure.

Questionnaire-based guidance is informational and depends on the answers provided. It may be incomplete or not fit a specific home setup.

Users are responsible for running AI HomeGuard only on systems, accounts, and networks they own or are authorized to assess. Do not use the project to inspect third-party systems, public targets, or environments where you do not have permission.
